About Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a field at the center of which is a 22-yard pitch with a wicket at each end, each comprising two bails balanced on three stumps.

The game is played in various formats, including Test matches, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20).

ICC

ICC (INTERNATIONAL CRICKET COUNCIL) is the global governing body of cricket, founded in 1909 by representative of Australia,england and south africa. ICC headquater is in DUBAI,UAE
